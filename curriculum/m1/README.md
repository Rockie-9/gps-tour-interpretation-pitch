# M1 · TORE 入門 · TORE basics · TORE 入門

## Phase
Explorer (Phase 1, week 1).

## Time budget
- Content: 6 hours.
- Validation: 1 day (write + deliver theme sentence; reviewed within 24h).

## Learning outcome
Trainee can write a one-sentence theme from a body of exhibit facts and deliver it in 30 seconds spoken.

## Maps to capabilities
Layer 02 capability **02 Theme Crafting** (primary); **01 Content Mastery** (prerequisite).

## Sample artifact (v0.1)
A one-page worksheet:
1. Source material (3 paragraphs of exhibit fact provided).
2. Trainee writes ONE theme sentence (≤ 24 characters TC / ≤ 20 words EN).
3. Trainee delivers spoken version (recorded; ≤ 30 seconds).

**SME-pending**: the worksheet template itself, the exemplar theme sentences from past trainees, and the recording rubric.

## Validation rubric
Three-state (per BARS):
- **Present**: Theme is propositional (has a verb, makes a claim), not just topical (a noun).
- **Partial**: Theme is propositional but obscured by jargon or list structure.
- **Absent**: Theme is a category name, not a claim.
