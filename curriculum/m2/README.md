# M2 · 嗓音與體能管理 · Voice & Stamina · 声と体力管理

## Phase
Explorer (Phase 1, week 2).

## Time budget
- Content: 4 hours classroom + 5 days self-practice.
- Validation: 5 consecutive end-of-shift voice tests.

## Learning outcome
Trainee can sustain audible, clear speech and steady posture across a 200-contact day without quality drift in the last hour.

## Maps to capabilities
Layer 02 capability **03 Voice & Stamina** (primary).

## Sample artifact (v0.1)
- Recorded baseline: 60-second exhibit description, AM start of shift.
- Recorded end-of-shift on days 1–5: same exhibit description.
- Posture self-photo (end of day 5) compared to baseline.

**SME-pending**: the voice-tone analysis criteria, the posture rubric, the breath/diaphragm exercises adapted from cabin-crew training (per §J hospitality anchor).

## Validation rubric
- **Present**: Voice quality on day-5 recording ≥ 90% of day-1 baseline on three measures: pitch stability, volume floor, articulation.
- **Partial**: 75–90% match; one measure drifts.
- **Absent**: < 75% on any measure.
