# M6 · 服務恢復 · Service Recovery · サービス回復

## Phase
Builder (Phase 2, weeks 7–8).

## Time budget
- Content: 16 hours (theory + role-play).
- Validation: mystery-visitor score ≥ 4/5.

## Learning outcome
After a service misstep, the trainee acknowledges, repairs, and rebuilds trust — without concealment or deflection. Closes scenario C1.

## Maps to capabilities
Layer 02 capability **09 Service Recovery** (primary); **07 Interactive Adaptation** (supporting).
Scenario coverage: **C1 Service Recovery**.

## Sample artifact (v0.1)
- 5 role-play scenarios per trainee, each with a different misstep class (wrong directions, factual error, broken accessibility, lost item, complaint escalation).
- Mystery-visitor visit by an L4+ assessor pretending to be aggrieved.
- Recovery scored on: acknowledgement clarity, repair adequacy, trust-rebuild evidence (visitor leaves at neutral sentiment or better).

**SME-pending**: the 5 role-play scripts + the public-apology-grammar primer.

## Validation rubric
- **Present**: Mystery visitor scores 4 or 5 on 5-point scale; debrief shows the trainee can articulate WHY each step worked.
- **Partial**: Score 3; trainee got the outcome but cannot articulate the why → retry after 2 weeks.
- **Absent**: Score ≤ 2 OR any concealment/deflection observed.

## Dependencies
M2 (voice — required to hold steady tone under challenge), M5 (cross-cultural — apology grammar varies).
