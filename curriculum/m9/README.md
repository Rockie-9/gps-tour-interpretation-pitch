# M9 · 帶人與認證 · Mentoring & Certification · 育成と認証

## Phase
Architect (Phase 3). **Required for L5 promotion.**

## Time budget
- Content: 40+ hours over 3–6 months (mentor a real trainee through real progression).
- Validation: mentee passes L2 certification under independent assessor.

## Learning outcome
Trainee (themselves L4+) mentors one new hire from L0 to L2; documents the journey; mentee passes L2 certification by an independent assessor.

## Maps to capabilities
All 12 Layer 02 capabilities at meta-level (mentor must articulate WHAT they're transmitting at each step).

## Sample artifact (v0.1)
- Mentor logs (one entry per week of mentoring, in the audit log via `OBSERVE_BARS` events).
- Mentee's milestone artifacts (M1 theme sentence, M3 wayfinding scripts, etc.).
- Mentee's L2 certification result.

**SME-pending**: mentoring playbook + week-by-week checkpoint structure.

## Validation rubric
- **Present**: Mentee passes L2 certification by an independent (not the mentor) assessor; mentor produces complete log; mentee debriefs and confirms mentoring quality ≥ 4/5.
- **Partial**: Mentee passes but mentor log is incomplete OR mentee debrief is 3/5.
- **Absent**: Mentee fails L2; mentor cannot articulate what went wrong; cannot retry without a different mentee.

## Dependencies
M7 (micro-experience), M8 (calibration). Mentor must hold L4 to attempt M9. **Completion of M9 is required (but not sufficient) for L5 promotion.**
